KEY FEATURES IMPLEMENTED
================================================================================

1. BALANCE CHECKER
   - Call API to verify current card balance
   - Display balance to customer
   - Update dispute form

2. FRAUD INTAKE SYSTEM
   - Customer-facing Service Portal form
   - Upload evidence (receipt/screenshot)
   - Automatic risk calculation
   - Dispatch to analyst

3. FRAUD RISK SCORING (6 Factors)
   - Amount risk (0-35 points)
   - Evidence quality (0-30 points)
   - Transaction time (0-12 points)
   - Volume/history (0-30 points)
   - Account age (0-25 points)
   - Description analysis (0-15 points)
   - Total: 0-100 score

4. INTELLIGENT ROUTING
   - Auto-approve low risk (< 40 score)
   - Route to analyst (40-60 score)
   - Route to manager (60-80 score)
   - Escalate critical (> 80 score)

5. DECISION WORKFLOW
   - Email notifications to analyst
   - Analyst reviews evidence
   - One-click approve/reject
   - Customer notification
   - Refund tracking

6. SLA TRACKING
   - 5-day standard SLA
   - 2-day for high-risk
   - 24-hour for critical
   - Auto-escalation if breached
   - SLA reminder notifications

7. ROLE-BASED ACCESS
   - Customer: See own disputes
   - Analyst: See assigned disputes
   - Manager: See high-risk disputes
   - Admin: See all

8. AUDIT TRAIL
   - Log all scoring decisions
   - Track all approvals/rejections
   - Compliance reporting
   - Full history for disputes
